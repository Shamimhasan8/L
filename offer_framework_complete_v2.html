Define THEIR Dream Outcome (Not Yours)
The formula: Get [Result] in [Time] without [Pain] · 6 worksheet fields
▼
Principle (from sheet)
CRITICAL REALITY CHECK: What YOU want: Cool ML projects, learning opportunities, work-life balance, good salary. What THEY want: Someone who reduces costs, ships without hand-holding, solves problems independently. This is the #1 mistake people make. The formula: "Get [Result] in [Time] without [Pain]" — this becomes your value proposition.

Choose Your Guarantee
Phase 3: Ownership · Risk reversal strategy · 2 fields: My guarantee + Why it reduces their risk
▼
A guarantee reverses the hiring risk. Instead of THEM taking a risk on you, YOU take on the risk. This flips the power dynamic completely.
3 options from the sheet (pick one)
30-day trial:"Start me for 30 days. If I have not delivered measurable value by day 30, you can exit with 2 weeks notice. No long-term commitment until proof exists."
Paid project:"Give me a real problem. I will solve it in 48–72 hours. You pay a fixed fee. If you like the quality, we continue. If not, you have the solution and owe nothing more."
Performance-based:"Tie my first raise to a specific production metric. If the system achieves under 500ms p95 latency and DER under 0.20 on your data within 60 days — milestone reached."
